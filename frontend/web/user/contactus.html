Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a reservation?

expand_more

To make a reservation, simply search for parking near your destination, select your desired parking spot, and complete the payment process. You'll receive a confirmation email with your reservation details.

Can I cancel my reservation?

expand_more

Yes, you can cancel your reservation up to 24 hours before your scheduled arrival time for a full refund. Cancellations made within 24 hours are non-refundable. Please check our cancellation policy for more details.

What payment methods do you accept?

expand_more

We accept all major credit cards, including Visa, MasterCard, and American Express. We also support payments through PayPal and Apple Pay for your convenience.

What if I arrive late?

expand_more

We understand that delays can happen. Your reservation is held for one hour after your scheduled arrival time. If you anticipate being later than that, please contact our support team to make arrangements.
